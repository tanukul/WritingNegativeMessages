Decision Point: Choosing a Buffer Statement

​You've decided to begin breaking the bad news to Kenisha with a buffer statement that will hopefully "soften the blow." You're aware that you can use a variety of buffer statements, such as expressing your appreciation, demonstrating that you understand the other person's needs or goals, or conveying your willingness to help.

Of the following options, which buffer statement would be the most appropriate for this situation?
