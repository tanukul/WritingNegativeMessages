You chose to begin your explanation with the statement, "The customers in the focus groups thought..."​. This was an incorrect choice. An ideal explanation defuses the emotions that accompany bad news and uses neutral language instead of accusations or blame. Telling Kenisha that the team is building a cruise ship when a rowboat will do is accusatory, and she is likely to feel blamed rather than respected. Another option would be a better choice.
